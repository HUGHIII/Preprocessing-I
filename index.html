Experience

I served as military tribune under my brother in law the famed Scipio Aemilianus during the the third Punic war. I was the first over the walls of the great city of Carthage ending the three year siege of the city. Upon returning I witnessed the lands owned by my fellow citizen soldiers in disrepair. Unable to restore their lands which had sustained them in the past they were forced to sell them for a mere weeks worth of grain! Who was it that exploited these circumstances? The wealthy patricians!
